About 12Stone

12Stone Church exists so future generations will know the rescuing hand of God.

We believe that every person matters to God and matters to us. #OneMatters

We believe God calls us to pursue him just as we are, inside and out, so we nurture a casual, fun and inviting atmosphere. As our guest, you can check out our services without intrusion and begin your spiritual journey at your own pace.
